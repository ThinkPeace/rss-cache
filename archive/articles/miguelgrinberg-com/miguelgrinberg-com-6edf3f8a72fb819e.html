This is a short blog post to announce that I'm migrating the site in which I host my paid courses to a new platform at https://learn.miguelgrinberg.com. If you have purchased a course or ebook directly from me, this article tells you how to transfer your account to the new site.

Why is the Courses site moving?

My Courses site has been hosted by Teachable all this time. Choosing Teachable as a hosting partner a few years ago seemed like the right decision, but over time this platform has stagnated, while the membership fees continued to increase year after year. My renewal date comes up in May, and this time the price is about four times what I paid last year. Four times!

As I'm sure you can imagine, the demand for learning materials is very low in the era of generative AI, so I cannot really justify renewing my Teachable site for another year. I expect that site will go offline sometime during May 2026 when my current membership expires, and at that point I'll have the courses.miguelgrinberg.com domain redirect to the new site at learn.miguelgrinberg.com.

Unfortunately I could not find any reasonable alternative to Teachable, so I decided to draw on my do-it-yourself roots and build my own, using my old friends Python and Flask (and with no help from generative AI, in case that matters to you as it does to me). Having my own platform allows me to keep my hosting costs under control, and protects me against yet another platform enshittifying on me down the line.

How to migrate your account

If you have an account on the Teachable site, you can now register a new account at the new website. Be sure to use the same email address you are using with Teachable, so that your purchases are matched to your new account. Once you verify your email address you will have access to the materials you own.

Even though I tried to make the transition from Teachable as straightforward as possible, I do not want to discard the possibility that I may have missed something. If you experience any trouble accessing your content on my new site or need help locating your account information please reach out and I'll be happy to assist.

New orders

For those who may still be interested in purchasing my ebooks or video courses, orders are now handled by Dodo Payments. This is a service that is in many ways similar to the much more popular Stripe, but in addition to processing online payments Dodo also takes care of collecting and remitting sales tax in the jurisdictions that require it, something that Stripe does not do and I have no interest in doing myself.
