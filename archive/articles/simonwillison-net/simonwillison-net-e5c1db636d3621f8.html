On the <dl> (via) I learned a few new-to-me things about the 

<dl>
element from this article by Ben Meyer:

  • A 
    <dt>
    can be followed by multiple
    <dd>
  • You can optionally group the 
    <dt>
    and
    <dd>
    elements in a
    <div>
    for styling - but only a
    <div>
    .
  • You can label them using ARIA.
  • They've been called "description lists", not "definition lists", since an HTML5 draft in 2008.

So this is valid:

<h2 id="credits">Credits</h2> <dl aria-labelledby="credits"> <div> <dt>Author</dt> <dd>Jeffrey Zeldman</dd> <dd>Ethan Marcotte</dd> </div> </dl>
