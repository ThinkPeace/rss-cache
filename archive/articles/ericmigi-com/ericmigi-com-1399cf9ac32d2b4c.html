The new Pebble app is live on iOS and Android

[2025-11-12]

It’s alive!#

Last month, we published the brand new Pebble app to the iOS App Store and Android Play Store. It works with all Pebbles, including OG Kickstarter Pebble, Pebble Steel, Pebble Time, Pebble Time Round, Pebble 2, Pebble 2 Duo and Pebble Time 2.

We're so happy to be breathing new life into a 13 year old product. Dig your old Pebble out of the drawer and use it with the new app rePebble.com/app

A few notes:#

  • We’ve still got a lot of things to work on in the app. See our software roadmap.
  • See our tips and tricksfor troubleshooting advice.
  • One major new improvement is the local speech-to-text processing option. Try it out!
  • The Bluetooth connection from this app to old Pebbles is not as reliable as the connection to new Pebbles (P2D and PT2) because we can't easily fix bugs in the software of the old watches. We're doing what we can. Please keep that in mind - try not to give it bad reviews based on how well it connects to old Pebbles.
  • We open sourced the library that powers this app (libpebble3), which is already being used in open source apps likeMicroPebble.GadgetBridgeis another good open source Pebble-compatible app and supports all Pebble watches.
